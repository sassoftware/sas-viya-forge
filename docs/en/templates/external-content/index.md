The SAS Content Assessment for PC Users is designed to evaluate and manage SAS content, specifically on Windows and Linux PCs. It focuses on identifying, cataloging and analyzing SAS content and assisting with migration planning. It is specifically aimed at SAS environments where users store their content, like SAS programs or Enterprise Guide Projects on a PC.  
It is a related tool to SAS 9 Content Assessment. SAS 9 Content Assessment Tool is a broader tool for assessing SAS 9 environments that include a metadata server.
